Get an invite to apply for probate online

Probate gives you the legal right to deal with a person’s property, money and possessions (their ‘estate’) after they’ve died.

It's not yet possible for everyone to make an application online.

We will invite you to try this new service if you can confirm that you have:

  • the original will of the person who died
  • a death certificate (this must be the original or a interim death certificate) from the coroner
  • completed an inheritance tax form
  • been named as an executor on the will, or an update to the will ('codicil')
  • tried to contact all the other executors named in the will, to tell them you're applying for probate and to find out if they want to apply
  • the mobile number and email of every executor who's applying for probate, so they can confirm online that the information you provide is correct
  • determined that all the executors are able to make their own decisions - if one of them is considered to be lacking in mental capacity you won't be able to apply online

The person who died must also have regarded England or Wales as their permanent home.
