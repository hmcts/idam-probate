About the executors

An executor is someone named in the will as being responsible for dealing with the estate.

Sometimes executors will be replaced by other executors. The will usually explains the circumstances in which an executor can be replaced, but reasons may include:

  • an executor dying
  • an executor becoming mentally incapacitated
  • someone being granted a power of attorney on behalf of an executor

The information we need

On the next page you’ll be asked to tell us about all the executors. This number must include everyone who:

  • is currently an executor
  • was previously an executor but has been replaced

We do not need to know about anyone who might become an executor in the future.
