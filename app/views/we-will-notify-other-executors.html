Notify the other executors who are applying for probate

The other executors who are applying for probate now need to make their legal declarations.

Once you've used this service to notify them, they'll each be sent an email telling them how to make their declarations.

A grant of probate can't be issued until all the applying executors legally declare that the information is correct.

Staying in contact

You'll need to stay in contact with the other applying executors throughout this stage of the application.

By staying in contact you'll:

  • know when everyone's made their declarations, so you can return to this service and submit your application
  • be able to discuss the information you've provided and make any changes if needed

If you change the information on your application

You can continue to make changes to your application until everyone has made their legal declaration.

If you make any changes, you'll need to contact the other applying executors to let them know the legal declaration has been updated and is ready for them to agree.

They can always see the most up to date version of the legal statement using the link we email them.
