Your account has been activated. You can now continue to apply for probate.

You have 6 months to complete your application.

Filling in your probate application

Use this link to sign in and continue your application:

https://www.probate.reform.hmcts.net/sign-in.

To make the application you'll need:

  • the original will of the person who has died
  • the death certificate
  • the completed inheritance tax form
  • to have contacted any other executors, to discuss whos applying, who isn't and reasons why they aren't applying
  • the mobile number and email of every executor who's applying for probate
    Your progress will be saved so you can leave and return to your application.

After you've completed your probate application

After you've submitted your online probate application, you'll need to send the death certificate, the original will and any other necessary documents to the probate registry office at:
