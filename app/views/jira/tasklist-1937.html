Apply for probate

Complete these steps to apply for probate.

  1. About the will and inheritance tax

    To complete this section you need:

    • the will
    • updates to the will (‘codicils’) if there are any
    • the IHT inheritance tax form

    Start

  2. About the executors and the person who has died

    To complete this section you need:

    • the will
    • updates to the will (‘codicils’) if there are any
    • death certificate

    If there's more than one executor applying for probate you need:

    • the mobile number and email address of everyone who's applying
    • to have discussed the application you're making with everyone who's applying

    You must complete the previous step before you can start this

  3. Review and confirm

    Please review the information entered

    You must complete the previous step before you can start this

  4. Order extra copies of the grant of probate

    Extra copies are 50p each. You can also order copies to use outside the UK if the person who died had assets abroad

    You must complete the previous step before you can start this

  5. Pay and submit

    The probate application fee is £215.00 for estates valued at £5,000 or over.

    There is no probate application fee for estates valued under £5,000.

    You must complete the previous step before you can start this

  6. Send documents

    You need to send:

    • documents including the original will and death certificate (the death certificate must be the original or a certified copy)

    We will check these supporting documents and issue your grant.

    You must complete the previous step before you can start this
