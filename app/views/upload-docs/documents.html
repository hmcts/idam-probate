How to take the picture
  1. Place the document on a flat service in a well lit room. Use a flash if you need to.
  2. Take a good quality picture of the entire document. You should be able to see its edges.
  3. Check that the image is in focus. You must be able to read all the writing, including any handwriting. Zoom in if you need to check this.
  4. Send the picture to this device (eg by email) and save it.

The image must be of the entire document and has to be readable by probate registry staff. You can upload most types of image.
