Apply for probate

Complete these steps to apply for probate.

  1. 1. About the will

    Answer questions about the will and any updates to the will (‘codicils’).

    Start

  2. 2. About the person who died

    Answer questions about the person who has died and their estate.

    The previous step must be complete before you can start this.

  3. 3. About the executors

    Answer questions about the executors.

    The previous step must be complete before you can start this.

  4. 4. Check your answers and make your legal declaration

    You need to carefully check the information you've given before making your legal declaration.

    The other applying executors can only make their legal declarations after you've made yours.

    The previous step must be complete before you can start this.

  5. 5. Order extra copies of the grant of probate

    Extra copies are 50p each. You can also order copies to use outside the UK if the person who died had assets abroad

    The previous step must be complete before you can start this.

  6. 6. Pay and submit

    The probate application fee is £215.00 for estates valued at £5,000 or over.

    There is no probate application fee for estates valued under £5,000.

    The previous step must be complete before you can start this.

  7. 7. Send documents

    You need to send:

    • documents including the original will and death certificate (the death certificate must be the original or a certified copy)

    We will check these supporting documents and issue your grant of probate.

    The previous step must be complete before you can start this.
