Apply for probate

To complete all the steps in your application you’ll need:

  • the will and any updates to it ('codicils')
  • the death certificate
  • information from the IHT form
  • to know which executors are applying for probate and their contact details

Once you’ve completed all the steps you should receive your grant of probate within 20 days.
